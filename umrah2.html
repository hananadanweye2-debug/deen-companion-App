🛫 Step 2:CHECKLIST, DUA & IHRAM

📦 Checklist Before Leaving Home

  • Ensure all documents are packed: passport, visa, ID, tickets.
  • Check that Ihram clothing and accessories (belt, bag) are ready.
  • Pack travel essentials: money, phone, charger, power bank, hygiene items.
  • Inform family if necessary and secure your home.
  • Mental preparation: make your intention in your heart for Umrah/Hajj.

👕 Wearing the Ihram

  • Men should wear the two white cloths (Rida and Izar) properly.
  • Women should wear modest clothing; regular clothing is allowed.
  • Ensure the Ihram is clean, comfortable, and secure for travel.
  • Make your intention (Niyyah) silently in your heart for Umrah/Hajj.
  • Once in the state of Ihram, avoid perfume and prohibited actions.

🏠 Dua When Leaving Home

بِسْمِ اللهِ تَوَكَّلْتُ عَلَى اللهِ وَلَا حَوْلَ وَلَا قُوَّةَ إِلَّا بِاللهِ

Transliteration:

Bismillahi, tawakkaltu ‘ala Allah, wa la hawla wa la quwwata illa billah

Meaning:

In the name of Allah, I place my trust in Allah, and there is no power or strength except with Allah.

✈️ Dua for the Journey

سُبْحَانَ الَّذِي سَخَّرَ لَنَا هَذَا وَمَا كُنَّا لَهُ مُقْرِنِينَ وَإِنَّا إِلَى رَبِّنَا لَمُنقَلِبُونَ

اَللّٰهُمَّ إِنَّا نَسْأَلُكَ فِي سَفَرِنَا هَذَا الْبِرَّ وَالتَّقْوَى وَمِنَ الْعَمَلِ مَا تَرْضَى

Transliteration:

Subhana allathee sakhkhara lana hatha wa ma kunna lahu muqrineen wa inna ila rabbina lamunqaliboon
Allahumma inna nas’aluka fi safarina hadha al-birra wa at-taqwa wa minal-amali ma tardha

Meaning:

Glory be to Him who has subjected this for us, and we could never have done it by ourselves, and surely to our Lord we shall return.
O Allah, we ask You in this journey for righteousness and piety, and for deeds that please You.
