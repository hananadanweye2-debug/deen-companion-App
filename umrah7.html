🕋 Step 7: Maqam Ibrahim

🔹 Overview

After completing the seven circuits of Tawaf, it is Sunnah to perform two Rak’ahs (units of prayer) behind or near Maqam Ibrahim (Station of Abraham). During this prayer, recite Surah Al-Kafirun in the first Rak’ah and Surah Al-Ikhlas in the second, following Surat Al-Fatiha. Afterward, drink Zamzam water.

👳 Men’s Ihram Coverage

Once Tawaf is completed, men should cover both shoulders with their Ihram cloth. This signifies the completion of the Tawaf and proper attire for the prayer at Maqam Ibrahim.

📝 Key Actions at Maqam Ibrahim

  • Locate the Spot: Move toward the back of Maqam Ibrahim to perform the prayer. If crowded, it is acceptable to pray anywhere in Masjid al-Haram.
    • وَاتَّخِذُوا مِنْ مَقَامِ إِبْرَاهِيمَ مُصَلًّى

    Transliteration: "Wattakhidhu min maqāmi Ibrāhīma muṣalla"

    Meaning: "And take the Maqam Ibrahim as a place of Salah."

    Prayer

    • Perform two rak’ahs of prayer after completing Tawaf.
    • It is recommended to recite Surah Al-Kafirun in the first rak’ah after Surah Al-Fatiha.
    • Recite Surah Al-Ikhlas in the second rak’ah after Surah Al-Fatiha.

    Zamzam Water

    • Drink Zamzam water available near the Tawaf area.
    • Take the opportunity to make personal du’a while drinking.

    Next Step

    • Proceed to Sa’ee, which involves walking between Safa and Marwa.

💡 Notes

It is not obligatory to be directly behind Maqam Ibrahim if it is crowded. Any place in the surrounding area is sufficient. Focus on your devotion and sincerity.
