🕋 Step 6: Tawaf in Detail

🔹 What is Tawaf?

Tawaf involves circling the Kaaba seven times in a counterclockwise direction, beginning and ending at the Black Stone (Hajar al-Aswad), while in a state of wudu. Each round is a personal prayer for forgiveness, dhikr (remembrance of Allah), or Quranic recitation, symbolizing submission to Allah's command.

📝 Preparation

  • Ensure you are in a state of wudu (ablution/purity).
  • Men should wear proper Ihram attire; women should wear modest clothing appropriate for Tawaf.
  • Mentally prepare yourself for spiritual focus and humility.

🏁 Beginning the Circuit

  • Start at the Black Stone corner (identified by a green light).
  • Face the Black Stone, point toward it with your right hand, and say “Allahu Akbar” (Allah is the Greatest).
  • Keep the Kaaba to your left throughout the rotation.

1️⃣ First Three Rounds

Men may walk briskly (Raml) between the Yemani Corner and the Black Stone if able. Women should walk at a normal pace. Focus on dhikr, personal du’as, or Quranic recitation.

2️⃣ Remaining Four Rounds

Continue walking normally while maintaining spiritual focus. There are no fixed supplications; make sincere personal du’a and remembrance of Allah throughout.

💡 Between Yemani Corner & Black Stone

Recite this sunnah dua during each round:

رَبَّنَا آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً وَفِي الْآخِرَةِ حَسَنَةً وَقِنَا عَذَابَ النَّارِ

Transliteration: Rabbana atina fid-dunya hasanatan wa fil-akhirati hasanatan waqina 'adhaban-nar

Meaning: Our Lord, give us good in this world and in the Hereafter and protect us from the Fire.

🙏 Dua for the Other Three Corners

At each of the other corners of the Kaaba, raise your hands and make a sincere personal du’a. You may ask for anything from your heart, in any language. Sincerity is what counts most.

💧 Wudu Breaks During Tawaf

If your wudu is broken or you feel the need to refresh it, step aside briefly and perform ablution. Return quickly to continue your Tawaf while maintaining focus on your devotion.

✅ Completing Tawaf

  • After completing seven rounds, offer two Rak’ahs of prayer behind or near Maqam Ibrahim (Station of Abraham).
  • Make final personal du’as and engage in dhikr.

⚠️ Key Rules

  • Keep the Kaaba on your left shoulder at all times.
  • Do not enter the Hijr Isma'il (semi-circular wall area).
  • If crowded, gesture towards the Black Stone instead of touching it physically.
