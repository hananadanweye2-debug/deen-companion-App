🕋 Step 1: Preparation, Forgiveness, and Debt

📍 Practical Concerns & Must-Have Items

  • Ihram clothing for men; women can wear regular modest clothing.
  • Ihram belt or hanging bag to carry essential items (money, passport, ID).
  • Comfortable shoes or sandals.
  • Personal hygiene items (towel, soap, tissue).
  • Device items (phone, charger, power bank, headphones).

💰 Outstanding Debts

Before starting Umrah, ensure that you have cleared any debts you owe to others. Paying off debts or making arrangements shows responsibility and removes distractions, so you can focus entirely on your spiritual journey.

🤲 Forgiveness

Seek forgiveness from those you may have wronged, and forgive others who may have wronged you. Entering Umrah with a clean heart and reconciled relationships strengthens your devotion and makes the experience spiritually uplifting.

🕊 Practicing Patience

Traveling for Umrah can be physically and emotionally challenging. Practice patience in crowds, long queues, and waiting times. Patience enhances your focus and humility, which are key virtues during pilgrimage.

⚠️ Avoid

  • Arguments, quarrels, or harsh language.
  • Using perfume once in the state of Ihram.
  • Overindulgence or wasting time on unnecessary activities.
