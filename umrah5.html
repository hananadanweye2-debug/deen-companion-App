🕋 Step 5: Tawaf

🏁 Starting Point

Begin your Tawaf at the corner of the Ka'bah where Hajar Al-Aswad (the Black Stone) is located. Face the Ka'bah with humility, raise your hands if possible, and say “Bismillah, Allahu Akbar” (In the name of Allah, Allah is the Greatest), then begin your Tawaf with focus and sincerity.

📍 Ihram Position

Men:

  • Wear the two white Ihram cloths properly, one covering the lower body and one over the upper body.
  • Uncover the right shoulder (placing the upper cloth under the right arm and over the left shoulder) throughout all seven circuits of Tawaf.
  • Go towards the corner where the Black Stone (Hajar Al-Aswad) is located.
  • Make your niyyah (intention) for Tawaf sincerely for the sake of Allah.
  • Begin Tawaf by facing the Ka'bah and moving in an anti-clockwise direction.
  • Maintain calmness, avoid pushing, and remain focused on worship.

Women:

  • Wear modest and loose-fitting clothing that fulfills the requirements of Ihram.
  • Keep the body fully covered, including the head, while keeping the face uncovered (unless necessary due to crowd conditions).
  • Go towards the area aligned with the Black Stone to begin Tawaf.
  • Make your niyyah (intention) sincerely for the sake of Allah.
  • Perform Tawaf in an anti-clockwise direction, maintaining dignity and composure.
  • Prefer the outer areas if needed for comfort and safety, especially in crowded conditions.

💎 Kissing / Touching Al-Hajar Al-Aswad

If possible, kiss or touch the Hajar Al-Aswad (Black Stone) as you pass it, while saying “Bismillah, Allahu Akbar.” If the area is crowded, simply gesture towards the Black Stone with your hand (istilam) and continue your Tawaf. In all cases, maintain humility, calmness, and focus, remembering that the act is symbolic—what matters most is sincerity of intention and devotion to Allah.

🕋 Additional Tawaf Guidance

  • Recite Talbiyah, Quranic verses, or personal supplications as you circumambulate the Ka'bah.
  • Complete seven rounds counter-clockwise, maintaining focus and humility.
  • Make du’a from your heart in any language; this is your personal time with Allah.
  • Men may run lightly between the Yemani corner and the Black Stone (Ramal) during the first three rounds if physically able.
  • Women should maintain a steady pace, focusing on devotion and prayer.
